A protocol for scalable, ecological production

Basis enables an economy that limits externalities by aligning personal gain with social well-being

The Basis project is an economic protocol: a specification that describes different types of economic entities (agents, resources, blocs) and defines the relationships and interactions between them. The project has a reference implementation and eventually plans to form the networks and build the interfaces that facilitate participation in the protocol.

How Basis works

Think of Basis like a network of people making things. To participate in this network, you play by the same rules as the other participants. Capitalism is much the same way. The interactions and relations in capitalism are enforced by the participants themselves. Basis shifts some of the ways things work in capitalism to create a healthier and more aware productive system, and does so without requiring some authority mandating the rules.

Transactional medium

Underneath all the economic operations in Basis is the transactional system. This allows producers ("blocs") to order products and services from each other, tracks labor and resource costs, and facilitates the other operations in the protocol. This is transparent to participants: all economic interactions are visible to all, creating a culture of openness and collaboration (with the exception of consumer purchases, which are entirely confidential). This does not eliminate competition, but allows space for other forms of production.

In-kind cost tracking

Basis replaces the concept of a price with separate collections of labor and resources. A chair no longer cost $35, but rather 4kg wood from a new growth forest in Oregon, 37 minutes of sawmill worker labor, 1.2 hours of trucker labor, 1.1L of diesel fuel, etc. This cost tracking in disaggregate allows much more accurate pricing when consumers make purchases than a simple, aggregate price. When producers order goods or services from each other, they are able to assign these costs to their products, which then transfer to the recipient. Using this method there is no profit at all, as all outputs match all inputs.

Currency system

Credits (₡) in Basis are paid as wages to workers and can be used for consumer purchases. Upon spending credits on consumer goods purchased from the producer network Basis forms, credits are destroyed. They do not circulate the economy and exist only outside the productive system. This creates a barrier around production which not only enforces its profitless nature but also allows for producers to price products separately from their cost without realizing those gains or losses directly.

Cybernetics

The profit mechanism is replaced with a more intentful approach that balances the well-being of workers, the health of the environment, and consumer relationships. This is done using cybernetics, which determines producer performance in the absence of profit, instead using the other available information in the protocol as a guideline. This allows automatic adjustment of the ongoing societal investment in a producer based on the demand for their products, the delta between their costs and their prices, etc. The cybernetics system can be thought of as a slow-moving gravitational pull toward benefiting society as a whole.

Basic income

Basic income is distributed to all participants periodically. Because the currency system is set up to not circulate the productive system, the basic income can be printed without taxation. Instead of taking from some and giving to others, it represents a social agreement that we are all productive to some extent, whether our wages reflect this or not. This not only acts as a simple safety net but also allows weakening the bond between productivity and economic survival, making the economy much more adaptable while also making a big shift towards free association.

Value systems

Severing the link between cost and consumption price allows for the use of different value systems. Maybe disabled people get much cheaper wheelchairs. Maybe we scrap currency altogether and try out a gift economy. However people wish to associate with each other in a productive sense is achievable in Basis because the currency and cybernetics systems can be adapted to fit many needs.
