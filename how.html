Basis forms a decentralized network of people actively engaging in a post-capitalist economy with one another.

The network acts as a foundation for members to provide goods and services to each other without needing the profit motive.
Read more »
Property in the network is shared by all members. Housing, factories, office space, and machinery are managed by those using them.
Read more »
The needs of the members are met by a Basic Income that grows into a living wage as the network becomes more self-sufficient.
Read more »
