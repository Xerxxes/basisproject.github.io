Basis is a Distributed Ledger Technology system. This means it creates a network where the members all participate under the same set of rules, providing a platform for equal opportunity. It also means that the things that happen on the network are transparent and open to review by the members, creating a culture of openness and honesty.

This type of distributed system acts like a self-enforcing constitution. Participants can trust that everyone is playing by the same rules,
Basis provides a foundation for engaging in production without profit, managing common property, and meeting the basic needs of members.
Bottom-up self-governance is at the core of Basis. Members have a voice in the things affecting them, whether in the workplace or the entire system.
{% include /svg/laptops.svg %}

In the beginning, the network will be overseen by a central organization. As the network's self-sufficiency increases, this organization's control cedes automatically. This setup helps protect the network in its infancy while ensuring its autonomy as time goes forward.

How Basis works: Part 2 - Membership and Companies

Network membership is available to anybody at a worker-owned company which uses Basis as the means of ordering/providing inventory and tracking labor costs.
