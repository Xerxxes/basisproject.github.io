Basis is a Distributed Ledger Technology system. This means it creates a network where all the members agree to participate under the same set of guidelines.

The network provides a foundation for engaging in production without profit, managing common property, and meeting the basic needs of members.
Bottom-up self-governance is at the core of Basis. Members have a voice in the things affecting them, whether in the workplace or the entire system.
This type of distributed system acts like a self-enforcing constitution. Participants can trust that everyone is playing by the same rules.
{% include /svg/laptops.svg %}

Everyone in the network shares the same view of what's happening. This means the things that happen on the network are transparent and open to review by the members, creating a culture of openness and honesty.

Knowledge and data are shared.

{% include leader.html text="How Basis works: Part 2 - Production without profit" image="/assets/images/port.jpg" large=true %}

Basis enables an economy where people are paid for producing things rather than for selling things. This shift reorganizes the productive system to focus on meeting needs instead of generating profit.
